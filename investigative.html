Investigative

Golden Glow Investigative & Protective Services, LLC can provide you with an array of investigative services. All Investigations are performed under terms of a contractual agreement and with strict adherence to client privacy. A complete comprehensive written report detailing investigative activities will be included in every assignment.

The client agrees to pay all expenses incurred by Golden Glow Investigative & Protective Services, LLC during the course of an investigation, and as agreed.

At the completion of an investigation, and with the forwarding of the complete written report, the client will be billed for all services and expenses. The bill is to be paid upon receipt of the final report, and no later than seven business days from the date of mailing.
